Most Complex Project — Global Revenue Planning & Monthly Financial Close
Designed and built a system replacing a fully spreadsheet-driven monthly close process for a company operating across 10+ markets and 15+ currencies. The forecast & actuals layer lets sales managers and finance teams create multi-dimensional revenue forecasts (by market, agency hierarchy, sales rep, deal type, and ad format) and automatically track actuals from CRM won deals against them — surfacing forecast vs. actuals variance in pivot reports ready for board-level presentations. The monthly close workflow coordinates three roles (operations, finance, management) through a structured sequence of activities auto-created at each stage transition, with configurable deadlines per market, escalation rules for overdue items, and a full audit trail in the chatter. The vendor statement management layer replaces email threads for incoming revenue statements: each statement moves through an activities-driven approval chain, generates vendor bills directly in Odoo Accounting, and automatically marks itself as paid when linked invoices are settled. The system handles 3 contract types, 10+ countries, and 15+ currencies simultaneously.
